Top 3 best anime: Ranked

These are the top 3 greatest animes ever made.

Fire force

This anime is the greatest and at the top of the list for a reason. The main character, Shinra Kusakabe goes from a weak fire user to a very OP character who destroys the world.

Jujutsu Kaisen

There is everything to be loved about this anime. As it has good story plot, nice character development, and excellent hand to hand combat.

Naruto

Naruto is also a good anime as it has good scenes and nice fighting techniques.
